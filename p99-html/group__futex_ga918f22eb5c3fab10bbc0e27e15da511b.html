void p99_futex_wait ( p99_futex volatile *  p00_fut)
related

Unconditionally wait for futex p00_fut.

This deschedules the calling thread until it is woken up by another thread through p99_futex_wakeup or equivalent.

Remarks
Such a waiting thread will not be subject to spurious wake ups when receiving signals.
